University Annual Reports

A written and photographic summary of the annual activities and accomplishments of students, faculty, and staff at the university.

My Roles

  • Art Direction
  • Brand Direction
  • Editing
  • Planning
  • Visual Design

Challenge

Annually present a number of unrelated topics, events, and accomplishments in a single publication with various audiences and stakeholders.

Solution

As the art director, I partnered with editors on my team to gather content, write, and design a thoughtful report with infographics and original photography.

Process

Content Creation

Multiple contributors, each with different goals, are asked to share information each year. This request is made in the form of a single questionnaire, which is shared with each department to ensure equal representation throughout the university.

Planning

Using the annual vision as a guide and content provided by returned questionnaires, an outline for the report is created and writing begins.

Collaboration

The team is challenged to make each report, containing similar content, people, and events, feel fresh and new. In an effort to be as efficient as possible, content is written for specific purposes, such as infographics, photo captions, or paragraphs, rather than edited to fit that purpose after the fact.

Conclusion

Despite the headache that each report causes, the result is consistently something to be proud of. After seeing a glimpse of its potential, contributors are more and more willing participate in the process each year and seem to provide more useful content, resulting in a report that gets better each year.
